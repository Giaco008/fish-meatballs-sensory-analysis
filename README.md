# 🐟 Tamarong Fish Meatballs Sensory Analysis  
### Development and Evaluation of Tamarong (Selar crumenophthalmus) Fish Meatballs Infused with Spinach and Moringa  

---

## 📌 Project Overview  
This project presents a comprehensive **sensory evaluation and statistical analysis** of Tamarong (*Selar crumenophthalmus*) fish meatballs infused with **spinach (*Spinacia oleracea L.*)** and **moringa (*Moringa oleifera*)**.  

The study evaluates how the addition of plant-based ingredients affects **sensory quality and consumer acceptability**, using **data visualization and statistical testing** performed in **Google Colab**.

---

## 🎯 Objectives  

### 1. Evaluate Sensory Quality in Terms of:
- Color  
- Aroma  
- Texture  
- Taste  
- Aftertaste  
- Overall Acceptability  

### 2. Statistical Objective:
- Determine whether there is a **significant difference among treatments** using **ANOVA and post-hoc analysis**  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights  

- ✅ The **control (T0)** achieved the highest acceptability scores  
- ✅ Treatments with spinach and moringa showed **slight reductions in sensory ratings**  
- ✅ Significant differences confirm that ingredient modification impacts perception  
- ✅ Some attributes (e.g., mouthfeel) showed less variation across treatments  

---

## 📈 Conclusion  

The results indicate that while spinach and moringa infusion introduces nutritional enhancements, it also leads to **statistically significant changes in sensory quality**.  

The **control formulation remains the most preferred**, suggesting that further optimization is needed to improve the acceptability of enriched variants.  

This highlights the importance of balancing **nutritional value and consumer preference** in food product development.
